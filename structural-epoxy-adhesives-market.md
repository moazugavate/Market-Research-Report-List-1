<p><strong>The Structural Epoxy Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural epoxy adhesives market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component adhesives are premixed formulations that require no additional components, simplifying application and storage; they cure through heat or moisture exposure. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed just before use, providing enhanced control over curing time and performance characteristics. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and aerospace industries for their strong bonding properties and durability.</p></p>
