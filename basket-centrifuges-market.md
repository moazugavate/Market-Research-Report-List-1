<p><ul><li>Vertical Basket Centrifuges</li><li>Horizontal Basket Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Basket centrifuges are industrial devices used for the separation of solids from liquids through centrifugal force. There are two main types: vertical and horizontal basket centrifuges. Vertical basket centrifuges feature a vertically oriented basket, optimizing gravity-driven sedimentation, making them suitable for smaller particle sizes and high-speed operations. In contrast, horizontal basket centrifuges have a horizontally positioned basket, which allows for larger capacities and easier cleaning, making them ideal for bulk processing applications and sludge treatment. Both types play critical roles in various industries.</p></p>
