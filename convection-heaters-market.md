<p><ul><li>Natural Convection Heater</li><li>Forced Convection Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The convection heaters market is primarily categorized into natural and forced convection heaters. Natural convection heaters rely on the natural upward movement of warm air, creating a gentle and quiet heating experience, ideal for small spaces. In contrast, forced convection heaters use fans to circulate air, delivering rapid and efficient heating in larger areas. Both types serve distinct needs, with natural convection being quieter and more energy-efficient for smaller rooms, while forced convection offers faster heating and broader coverage.</p></p>
