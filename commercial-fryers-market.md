<p><p>The commercial fryers market primarily comprises two main types: countertop and floor-standing fryers. Countertop fryers are compact, designed for limited spaces, and ideal for smaller establishments, providing versatility and ease of use. They are suitable for low to moderate volume frying. In contrast, floor-standing fryers are larger, often featuring advanced capabilities for high-volume cooking, making them suitable for busy kitchens like restaurants and catering services. These fryers offer enhanced cooking efficiency, durability, and often come with multiple baskets for simultaneous frying.</p></p>
