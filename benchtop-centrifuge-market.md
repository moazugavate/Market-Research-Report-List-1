<p><strong>The Benchtop Centrifuge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Benchtop Centrifuge</li><li>Isopycnic Benchtop Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop centrifuge market comprises various types, primarily including differential and isopycnic centrifuges. Differential benchtop centrifuges utilize varying centrifugal forces to separate substances based on density differences, making them ideal for routine laboratory tasks. In contrast, isopycnic benchtop centrifuges enable the separation of particles by achieving equilibrium densities in a gradient medium, ensuring precise separation of biomolecules and macromolecules. Both types provide essential tools for biochemistry, molecular biology, and clinical diagnostics, enhancing research efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
