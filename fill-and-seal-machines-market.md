<p><p>Fill and seal machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their construction and operational orientation. Vertical machines utilize a top-down approach, ideal for packaging liquids and granules, ensuring efficient filling and sealing in a compact footprint. In contrast, horizontal machines operate with products moving along a flat plane, accommodating larger packages and various materials. Each type serves distinct industries, with vertical machines often found in food and pharmaceuticals, while horizontal machines cater to diverse consumer goods applications.</p></p>
