<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The curved escalator market features two primary operating types: isokinetic operation and frequency conversion. Isokinetic operation maintains a consistent speed throughout transport, ensuring smooth passenger flow and enhanced safety. In contrast, frequency conversion technology allows for adjustable speed variations, optimizing energy efficiency and accommodating varying passenger loads. Both types aim to improve user experience and promote efficient movement in spaces with limited room, such as shopping malls and transportation hubs, catering to diverse architectural designs and urban environments.</p></p>
