<p><p>The flat stove market comprises primarily two types: Deep Well Type and Side Suction Type stoves. Deep Well Type stoves feature a recessed cooking area, allowing for the use of larger pots and even heat distribution, making them suitable for various cooking styles. In contrast, Side Suction Type stoves incorporate lateral ventilation to improve airflow, effectively managing smoke and odors. Both types cater to different culinary needs and preferences, enhancing efficiency and user experience in kitchen environments.</p></p>
