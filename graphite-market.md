<p><p>The graphite market comprises two primary types: natural graphite and synthetic graphite. Natural graphite is sourced from mining operations and features distinct flake, lump, or amorphous varieties, catering to diverse industrial applications, such as batteries and lubricants. In contrast, synthetic graphite is manufactured through high-temperature processes, providing customizable properties and higher purity levels, making it ideal for advanced applications like electric vehicle batteries and specialized electrodes. Both market segments are vital for supporting technological advancements and energy solutions.</p></p>
