<p><p>The bio plasticizers market consists of various types, each offering unique properties for enhancing biodegradable plastics. Epoxides are used to improve flexibility and thermal stability. Sebacates provide excellent low-temperature performance and compatibility. Succinic acid acts as a versatile, renewable alternative, enhancing mechanical properties. Citrates are known for their non-toxicity and biodegradability, making them suitable for food packaging. Glycerol esters serve as multifunctional agents, improving processing and durability. Other bio plasticizers include natural oils and starch derivatives, expanding application potential.</p></p>
