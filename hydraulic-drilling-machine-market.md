<p><p>The hydraulic drilling machine market comprises various types including rotary drills, impact drills, and compound drilling rigs. Rotary drills utilize a rotating bit to penetrate materials, making them ideal for deep hole drilling. Impact drills generate rapid, hammering action, enhancing drilling efficiency, particularly in hard substrates. Compound drilling rigs combine features of both rotary and impact mechanisms, offering versatility for various drilling tasks. These machines are essential in construction, mining, and oil exploration, facilitating efficient and effective drilling operations.</p></p>
