<p><ul><li>Metal Matrix Composites</li><li>Polymer Matrix Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The functional composites market comprises primarily two types: metal matrix composites (MMCs) and polymer matrix composites (PMCs). MMCs combine metals with other materials to enhance strength, durability, and thermal resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. PMCs utilize polymers as a matrix to provide lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and versatile materials suited for various industries like construction and electronics. Both types are integral in developing advanced materials that meet high-performance requirements across diverse sectors.</p></p>
