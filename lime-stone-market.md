<p><p>The limestone market is broadly categorized into High-Calcium Limestone and Magnesian Limestone. High-Calcium Limestone contains over 95% calcium carbonate, making it ideal for industrial applications like cement production, construction, and chemical manufacturing due to its purity and reactivity. Magnesian Limestone, with 5-35% magnesium carbonate, is valued for its durability and is commonly used in agriculture, steel production, and environmental applications like flue gas desulfurization. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their unique chemical compositions and functional properties.</p></p>
