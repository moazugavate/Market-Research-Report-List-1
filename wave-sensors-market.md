<p><strong>The Wave Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seabed</li><li>Sea Surface</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave sensors market can be categorized into three main types: seabed, sea surface, and others. Seabed sensors are deployed on the ocean floor to monitor wave patterns and sediment dynamics, providing critical data for offshore activities. Sea surface sensors are located above water, capturing real-time information on wave heights and frequencies, essential for navigation and marine weather forecasting. The "others" category includes specialized sensors used in unique environments, such as estuaries or coastal regions, tailored for specific research or industrial applications.</p></p>
