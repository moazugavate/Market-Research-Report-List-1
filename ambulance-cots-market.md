<p><p>The ambulance cots market is primarily divided into two types: retractable head and unretractable head cots. Retractable head cots feature adjustable head sections, allowing for customized positioning of patients, enhancing comfort and ease of access for medical personnel. In contrast, unretractable head cots have a fixed head position, providing a more stable base but less flexibility in patient positioning. Both types play crucial roles in patient transport, influencing ease of use and overall efficiency in emergency medical situations.</p></p>
