<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Oil Immersed Type</li><li>VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Type)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium voltage transformers market is primarily categorized into three types: Dry Type, Oil Immersed Type, and Vacuum Pressure Impregnated (VPI) Type. Dry Type transformers use air for cooling and are ideal for indoor installations, offering safety from fire hazards. Oil Immersed transformers utilize mineral oil for cooling, providing efficient thermal management, suitable for outdoor applications. VPI transformers combine the benefits of both, featuring enhanced insulation and moisture protection, thus extending lifespan and reducing maintenance needs. These types cater to various operational environments and applications.</p></p>
