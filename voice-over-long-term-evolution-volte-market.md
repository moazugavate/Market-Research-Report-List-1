<p><p>Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) is a technology that enhances voice calling over LTE networks. It encompasses several market types: </p><p>1. VoIP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS) relies on IMS architecture for voice services.</p><p>2. Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB) enables fall back to 2G/3G for voice calls.</p><p>3. Dual Radio/SV LTE supports simultaneous voice and data over LTE.</p><p>4. Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VoLGA) utilizes generic access for voice services.</p><p>5. Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC) ensures seamless call transitions between LTE and legacy networks.</p></p>
