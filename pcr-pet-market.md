<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PCR PET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>m-PCR(Mechanical PCR)</li><li>c-PCR(Chemical PCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR PET market includes m-PCR (Mechanical PCR) and c-PCR (Chemical PCR) as key types. m-PCR involves mechanically recycling PET by cleaning, shredding, and reprocessing it into new products, maintaining its polymer structure. It is cost-effective and widely used for packaging and textiles. c-PCR uses chemical processes to break PET into monomers, which are then repolymerized, producing virgin-like PET suitable for food-grade applications. Both methods support sustainability, with m-PCR focusing on efficiency and c-PCR on high-quality, reusable PET for sensitive applications.</p></p>
