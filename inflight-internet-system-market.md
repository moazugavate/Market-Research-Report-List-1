<p><strong>The Inflight Internet System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inflight internet system market includes three primary types: ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG systems utilize ground-based towers to provide connectivity, offering lower latency and cost-effective solutions primarily over land. In contrast, Ka Band Satellite systems offer higher data rates and coverage over vast geographic areas, ideal for transcontinental flights, while Ku Band Satellite systems provide reliable connectivity, especially for international travel, albeit with slightly lower speeds compared to Ka Band.</p></p>
