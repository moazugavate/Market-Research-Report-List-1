<p><strong>The Wet Electric Shaver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ratory Shaver</li><li>Reciprocating Shaver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wet electric shaver market is primarily divided into two types: rotary shavers and reciprocating shavers. Rotary shavers feature circular blades that move in a circular motion, adapting to the contours of the face for a close shave. They are ideal for longer hair and are often preferred for dry and wet shaving. Conversely, reciprocating shavers utilize lateral-moving blades, providing a direct and efficient cut, making them suitable for shorter hair. Both types offer distinct advantages based on user preferences and shaving styles.</p></p>
