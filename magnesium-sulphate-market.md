<p><ul><li>Heptahydrate (Epsom Salt)</li><li>Anhydrous (Calcined Kieserite)</li><li>Monohydrate (Kieserite)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnesium Sulphate market comprises three primary types: Heptahydrate (Epsom Salt), Anhydrous (Calcined Kieserite), and Monohydrate (Kieserite). Heptahydrate, commonly known as Epsom Salt, is widely used in agriculture, personal care, and medicine due to its high solubility. Anhydrous, or Calcined Kieserite, is a dehydrated form used in industrial applications and as a drying agent. Monohydrate, or Kieserite, is a naturally occurring mineral used in fertilizers and animal feed for its magnesium and sulfur content. Each type serves distinct industries based on its properties and applications.</p></p>
